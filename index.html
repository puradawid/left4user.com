Why it is better than normal testing

Left4User is a solution that allows you to reduce cost of production testing. If the quality of your product is what you really care about, it is better to get all user's insights noted down instead of trying to involve manual testers to find out issues that they are not really doing anyway.

We care about security. Data remains encrypted over all channels and you can select which data is too vulnerable to be processed by out AI engine. You will have the power to decrypt that data on your side (RSA).
